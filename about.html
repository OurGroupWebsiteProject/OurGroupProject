Who Are We:

Fly me is a travel agency offering a wide range of services that can meet your needs in ticketing and hotel reservations all over the world. It has won the trust of the various service providers with whom a close partnership has been established: hoteliers, transporters, restaurateurs through its reliability and seriousness. Fly me is a multidisciplinary travel agency.

Our Goal:

Our mission is to provide our customers with a high quality service allowing us to achieve the expected performance while respecting their expectations.

Our Values:

Excellence, Commitment, Transparency and Vision

  • Excellence: We are convinced that the success of a brand is not limited to the quality of its customer service, nor to the growth of its turnover, nor to its ability to develop a customer culture. It is the combination of these three facets that allows him to achieve excellence.
  • Commitment: Approach to building lasting relationships with our customers on the one hand, and development with our employees on the other.
  • Transparency: Involvement of clients in all phases of the project
