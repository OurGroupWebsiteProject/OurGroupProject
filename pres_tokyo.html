About us

Japan's top tourist destination Located around the eponymous bay, Tokyo is also the most visited city for tourists traveling to Japan, which seems logical given the fascination it provides by its great permanent gap between electric effusions and charm of its thousand villages.

Located in Tokyo, within 5 km of Hatomori Hachiman Shrine and 6 km of Min-On Concert Association, Mitsui Garden Hotel Jingugaien Tokyo Premier offers a restaurant, fitness centre and free WiFi. It is 6 km from Tonomine Naito Shrine, Meiji Memorial Picture Gallery and Sato Art Museum. Jingu Gaien Ginkgo Avenue and Meiji Jingu Gaien Resort are 7 km from the hotel.
