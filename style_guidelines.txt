Overall Look and Feel of TrAgent
================================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist and flat. We will avoid 3d buttons and 3d metal looks.
The website should be calm and gentle for the user.

Colours
========
For TrAgent, colours represent harmony and a way to attract people, the colors are eye-catching.
These are our color swatches:

- #B19CD7
- #5DBCF9
- #CACACA
- #rgb(255,255,255)
- #rgba(0,0,0,0.8)
- #f2f2f2
- #000000

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a 'flat' design, which adheres to the overall look and feel.


Typography
==========
Maghrib font is used to create something different from the other travel websites that use a regular font.

Links
=====
The links are part of the images that scroll and represent the cities, when you press on an image it will guide you directly to the corresponding page.

Buttons
=======
The buttons appear in white to be visible with the blue color of the page and create harmony.
